Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A classic and simple Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced garlic and chili flakes, saut√© until garlic is golden brown.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add to the pan. Toss well to coat the pasta.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley and Parmesan cheese if desired.

Caprese Salad

A fresh and simple salad made with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 250g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating between the two.
  2. Tuck basil leaves in between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
